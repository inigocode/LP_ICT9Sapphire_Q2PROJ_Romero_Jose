About Mangiamo Italiano

Our story starts on June 15, 1983 when a group of our three founding nonnas came together with a big idea.
Alessia Rossi, Anna Accetta, Bianca Ferrari came together to start Mangiamo Italiano, literally meaning "We eat Italian(food)".
Initially starting in Naples, Southern Italy, it gradually spread throughout Italy and the world.
Mangiamo Italiano now stretches from the star-studded city of Los Angeles to the modern Ginza district in Tokyo.
This website is to help you learn how to cook our recipes and for you to submit recipes to us direcly.

Visit us today at your local Mangiamo Italiano. Learn how to dine and cook with us!
